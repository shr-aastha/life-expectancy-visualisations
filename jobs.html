  1. Data Quality Manager

    Job Reference Number: AQ358

    Position Title: Data Quality Manager

    Position Summary: The Data Quality Manager serves as the in-house data expert, comfortable working with data and ensuring that data quality processes and related business processes are implemented. They will be responsible for independently defining the standard of data quality and develop a data strategy to enhance the quality of data across the entire organisation.

    Salary Range: $95,084 - $151,903 p.a. + 15% super + benefits

    Reporting to:Business Intelligence Officer

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Develop and implement a data quality strategy within the organisation
    • Collaborate with business stakeholders to define and execute a model for governing data management along with data quality standards.
    • Assist the Business Intelligence Officer to capture business requirement for data management functionality that can be deployed at the technology layer and enhance efficiency of the business
    • Report to the stakeholders to ensure departments are capable of delivering the necessary reports to drive business development
    • Design and implement data quality controls and automated validation checks.
    • Monitor data quality to ensure data accuracy and define data quality targets, alert thresholds and escalation conditions, and procedures
    Essential Skills and Attributes
    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Statistics, Data Management, or a related field.
    • Minimum experience of 7 years in data quality management or a similar data-related role
    • Strong understanding of document management systems and customer relationship managements tools, essentially Microsoft Dynamics and Salesforce
    • Proficiency in SQL, Python and R with a strong understanding of database and schema design
    • High attention to detail with excellent analytical skill.
    • Excellent written and verbal communication skills for communicating with partners and stakeholders with an ability to influence business interactions
    Preferable Skills and Attributes
    • Master's degree in Data Science or a related field
    • Experience in business analysis
    • Experience in medical/pharmaceutical field
    • Knowledge of data visualisation tools preferrably Tableau or Power BI

  2. Penetration Tester

    Job Reference Number: WA567

    Position Title: Penetration Tester

    Position Summary: The Penetration Tester will be responsible of conducting penetration tests on the organisations's ICT applications and gateway infrastructure. They will work closely with the Senior Penetration Tester and other security engineers to develop plans for penetration tests.

    Salary Range: $85,084 - $102,586 p.a. + 15% super + benefits

    Reporting to:Senior Penetration Tester

    Key Responsibilities:
    • Conduct penetration tests on the organisation's ICT applications and across a variety of web, mobile and wireless environments
    • Assist the Senior Penetration Tester in scoping and planning penetration test procedures and frameworks
    • Clearly ocument and report findings to technical and non-technical stakeholders
    • Conduct comprehensive security audits and employ ethical hacking to identify vulnerabilities and examine the security of the ICT systems
    • Recommend technical solutions to handle the detected vulnerabilities and enhance security of the ICT infrastructures
    Essential Skills and Attributes
    • Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, Information Technology, or related field.
    • Minimum experience of 3 years in penetration testing of similar cybersecurity role
    • Strong understanding of information security technologies and concepts
    • Proficiency in scripting languages, preferrably Python
    • Experience of penetration testing on web, mobile, cloud and hardware platforms
    • Excellent documentation and reporting ability
    Preferable Skills and Attributes
    • Master's degree in Cybersecurity or a related field
    • Experience in banking or insurance field
    • Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) certificate is highly regarded
